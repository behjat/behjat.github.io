Behjat Siddiquie

Email: behjats at gmail dot com
I am a Research Scientist at Amazon, working on Amazon Go, a no checkout grocery store. Previously, I was a Senior Computer Scientist at SRI International. I completed my PhD from the University of Maryland, College Park. In my thesis, I addressed the problem of efficiently retrieving images from large databases based on complex and multi-modal queries. Before that, I completed my BTech. from the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay.
